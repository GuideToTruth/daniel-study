Study Scope: The Book of Daniel

Our objective is to understand the prophetic timeline and the historical accuracy of the scriptures, centered on the King James Version.

Weekly Format (60 Minutes)

  • Opening Prayer: 2 Minutes
  • Scripture Reading (KJV): 10 Minutes
  • Teaching & Historical Context: 35 Minutes
  • Group Discussion: 15 Minutes

Curriculum Overview

  • Chapter 1: The Test of Loyalty & Health
  • Chapter 2: The Great Image & World Empires
  • Chapter 3: The Fiery Furnace
  • Chapter 4: The King's Madness
  • Chapter 7-12: The Prophetic Visions
