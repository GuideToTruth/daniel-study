How to Join

Joining on Google Meet

No account needed — just click the link and you are in

💻

On a Laptop or Computer

Windows, Mac, or Chromebook

  1. Click the Google Meet link below or shared on Facebook/WhatsApp
  2. Your browser opens the meeting — no download needed
  3. Type in your name when asked
  4. Allow microphone and camera access when prompted
  5. Click "Ask to join" — the host will let you in
📱

On a Phone or Tablet

iPhone, Android, or iPad

  1. Tap the Google Meet link below or shared on Facebook/WhatsApp
  2. Download the free Google Meet app from your app store — or it may open in your browser directly
  3. Tap "Join as guest" and enter your name
  4. Allow microphone and camera access when asked
  5. Tap "Ask to join" — done!
